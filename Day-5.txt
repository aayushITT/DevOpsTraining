CIDR Notation for Given IP Address

Given:
- IP Address: 135.1.1.25
- Subnet Mask: 255.255.248.0

Step 1: Convert Subnet Mask to Binary
255.255.248.0 in binary:
11111111.11111111.11111000.00000000

Step 2: Count the Number of 1s
The number of 1s in the subnet mask = 21

Step 3: Write in CIDR Notation
CIDR notation format:
IP Address / Number of 1s in Subnet Mask

Final CIDR Notation:
135.1.1.25/21
